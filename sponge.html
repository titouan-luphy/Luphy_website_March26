Where the sea
meets pure craft.

For centuries, the warm waters of the Aegean Sea have cradled one of nature's most remarkable organisms: the sea sponge. Spongerie works directly with third-generation sponge divers on the Greek islands of Kalymnos and Rhodes.

Each sponge is hand-selected at the optimal maturity stage, then naturally dried and trimmed — never bleached, never processed with harsh chemicals.

From Sea to Skin

The journey of
every sponge.

01

Deep Dive Harvest

Our divers hand-harvest sponges at depths of 5–15 metres, selecting only those that have reached full maturity — leaving young sponges to continue growing.

02

Sun Drying

Harvested sponges are spread on open decks and dried naturally in the Mediterranean sun for several weeks, preserving their natural fibres intact.

03

Hand Selection

Each sponge is individually examined for quality, softness, and texture. Only the finest specimens make it into the Spongerie collection.

04

To Your Door

Carefully packaged in recycled materials and shipped directly from our atelier in Athens, your sponge arrives ready to transform your daily ritual.
